for you, always —

i sometimes carry a quiet fear that in showing you these simple things — the little gifts of what i think is best — i might slowly and unintentionally drift away from the grander things you imagine.

i know how deeply you still love and value even the smallest gestures, and how no expensive things could ever replace the warmth they bring.

i find myself adjusting to what feels good and true, yet the thought of doing something that doesn't quite sit right with your heart frightens me. i worry so softly that i might accidentally steer us into a space that turns you away.

especially with the times where i've heard how easily you change your mind, how easily you get sensitive and emotional. but still, i know you carry so many things, both within you and around you, that i have yet to fully see or understand.

efforts are efforts, no matter how grand or small they might seem to anyone else. mine bring me a quiet peace, just knowing they might leave you feeling happy and secure. may God gently soften your stubborn ways, helping you lean in and listen to me more often, and may He take away that little laziness, so you will always remember to eat your meals on time.

i love you with all that i am, and i will love you for exactly who you are, embracing every single thing you have ever walked through — and all that you are still walking with, silently from me.

with everything i have ✦
